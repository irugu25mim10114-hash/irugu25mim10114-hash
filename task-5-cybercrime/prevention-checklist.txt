## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Always verify the sender before clicking on any link or email.
- Do not share passwords, OTPs, or personal details with anyone.
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication for better security.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown attachments.
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly.

## UPI / Financial Safety Tips:
- Never approve unknown payment requests in UPI apps.
- Double-check UPI IDs and phone numbers before sending money.

## Reporting Cybercrime:
